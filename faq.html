FAQ ( Frequently Asked Question )


Account



1. How to register as a member?

You may follow steps below to register:

a Open link : http://127.0.0.1:5500/Register.html
b Fill in your username
c Fill in your email address
d Fill in your password
e Click Register and you will receive an email notification


2. How do I change the information in my account?

Not to worry, you may call our 24 hours Customer Care line or drop a message at : //127.0.0.1:5500/Contact_Us.html
We will assist you as soon as possible.


3. How can I order without an account?

You need to create an account before placing an order. To register, kindly register on this link: http://127.0.0.1:5500/Register.html


4. I have submitted my online account registration but there is no verification email received. What should I do?

After submitting your account registration, an email with a verification link will be sent to you. If you did not receive the email in your Inbox, please check the spam or junk folder too.


5. I found the verification email but the authentication link in the email is not active/working. What should I do?

The link provided in the email will auto expire after 24 hours. If the link has expired, you may re-attempt to register again.


6. How to get the Tax Invoice of my purchase? Can I download my Tax Invoice?

Not to worry, you may call our 24 hours Customer Care line.


Order



1. How do I buy from HABABA?

You may place an order via our website (using desktop/pc/tablet/mobile phone). Use the search bar to find the products that you want to buy.


2. How do I know if my Order has been confirmed?

You will receive an email notification once your order has been confirmed or you will receive a SMS on your registered mobile number.


3. Does adding an item to my Cart mean that I've already booked/reserved it?

No, adding an item to your Shopping Cart allows you to continue shopping before you proceed to check out and make payment. You can keep the added items in your shopping cart for 7 days, while stock is still available. If the stock has run out, you will not be able to proceed with order check out.


4. How do I cancel my order?

You may cancel your order within 2 hours from order placed by visiting our website. Once you have logged in, go to MY ORDERS and all you have to do is click on the CANCEL button, select a reason and submit your order cancellation request. However, since change of address is not allowed, please ensure to select other reason to cancel order.


Payment



1. Can I pay with COD(Cash on Delivery)

Sorry, We are only providing credit/debit card payment.


2. How do I know if my payment is successful?

After ordering, a notification SMS/email will be sent upon successful payment.


3. My payment was charged but order was unsuccessful. What should I do?

First, please check your credit card or bank account statement to verify if there are any charges being imposed for the unsuccessful transaction. If yes, please send a copy of the respective statement that shows your full name, credit card number, transaction date and amount charged to : http://127.0.0.1:5500/Contact_Us.html and we will assist you further.


Delivery



1. Can I arrange for delivery overseas?

We do deliveries with all the country that we collab with.


2. Can I collect my orders from HABABA directly?

This option is not available currently. Our delivery partner will deliver your orders to you at your preferred delivery address.


3. Do you deliver during weekends and holidays?

Unfortunately, our courier partners are unable to deliver during weekends or public holidays. Please expect to receive your parcel on working days only.


4. My order status says ‘Delivered’ but I haven't received my item. What should I do?

A.  Check if someone else at your address has accepted it.
B.  Contact the courier. Ask if they have additional information about the package's location. Make sure you have your tracking number or delivery confirmation number in hand.
C.  Alternatively, you could also contact us at : http://127.0.0.1:5500/Contact_Us.html for further assistance.


Return and Refund



1. How do I request for return?

Within 10 days from the order receipt date, you may request for return through your online account. Alternatively, you may contact us on :http://127.0.0.1:5500/Contact_Us.html


2. Do I have to pay anything when I return an item?

No, there will be no additional charges for any return or refund process within the 10 days policy.


3. What should I do if I received incomplete item?

Do check the parcel received and identify the missing items and/or incomplete parts. Once you have checked, please contact us at : http://127.0.0.1:5500/Contact_Us.html to inform your issue and indicate which items are missing. A report will be created and our person in charge will get in touch with you within 1 to 2 working days.

Alternatively, subject to product return/exchange eligibility, you may return your product for an exchange or refund within 10 days from delivery via your online account. Once you have logged in, go to MY ORDERS and click on the RETURN button. We will be in touch to arrange for pick-up by our Delivery Partner.

While waiting to be contacted, please repack the item using its original packaging, complete with full accessories, warranty cards (if any) and do not use or consume the goods received.
