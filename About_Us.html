About Company Page


Hababa is an online shopping site which focuses mainly on e-commerce, Hababa was launched in Malaysia
in 2015 by Mr. Chia Jun Kang, and later expanded its reach to Singapore, Thailand, Taiwan, Indonesia,
Vietnam, Philippines and Brazil. Hababa provides a platform which allows customers to buy goods or
services from sellers over the Internet using a web browser or our app.


It also allows sellers to sell their products through Hababa. Due to the mobile and social element built
within the concept, Hababa has become the most popular online shopping platform in Asia. Everyone likes
to buy products with low prices. The users can check out our app for some great deals where we offer them
products at affordable prices.


Sometimes we will release some free vouchers so that the users can claim it and use it. We also have
massive sales and offers during major festivals like Chinese New Year, Hari Raya and others.
Let us not forget about our big sales campaigns such as 9.9 Super Shopping Day, 10.10 Brand Awesome,
11.11 Shop ‘n’ Roll and 12.12 Pre-Christmas Sales. This will make the price even cheaper and the buyer
will feel every cent they spent is well worth it.

Our Values


Hababa has also collaborated with local courier companies. For example, Poslaju, DHL Express, NinjaVan, J&T, and others. This will allow the buyers to enjoy low shipping costs and if you have spent over a certain amount and claimed our free shipping voucher, you can get it delivered to your doorsteps for free! For sellers, the shipping fee is free.
